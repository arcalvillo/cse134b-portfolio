Qwen3-4B Math Reasoning

Kaggle Competition · 2026

Overview

This competition challenges participants to improve the mathematical reasoning capabilities of a small-sized language model — specifically Qwen3-4B — using only model-intrinsic methods such as prompt engineering, supervised fine-tuning, and reinforcement learning.

Reliable mathematical reasoning unlocks the potential for LLMs to accelerate scientific discovery, automate complex quantitative analysis, and serve as accessible problem-solving tools across STEM disciplines. Approaches are evaluated on unified accuracy across problems ranging from high-school to graduate level.

Approach

My workflow has focused on iterative experimentation across three tracks:

  1. Baseline evaluation of the model on math benchmarks
  2. Prompt engineering to elicit chain-of-thought reasoning
  3. Supervised fine-tuning on curated math datasets
  4. Reinforcement learning for further reasoning improvements
