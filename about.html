About Me

Brief Description

I'm a Computer Science student at UC San Diego with a growing interest in machine learning, applied AI, and full-stack web development. I like problems that sit at the intersection of theory and something you can actually ship — the kind where the math matters but so does the user experience.

Outside of code, I spend time volunteering with animals and photography. I believe the best engineers stay curious about things beyond engineering, and both of those hobbies keep me grounded.
