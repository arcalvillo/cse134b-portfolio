About Me

Brief Description

I'm a Computer Science student at UC San Diego with a growing interest in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and full-stack web development. I'm drawn to exploring how Machine Learning can be applied to solving real issues — from making everyday tools smarter to tackling problems that matter beyond the tech industry. I'm especially interested in how we can design more efficient AI model data centers that are environmentally sustainable for all creatures who share this planet.

Outside of code, I spend my time speaking for the voiceless: advocating for animal rights to protect some of the most vulnerable victims of violence. I have fostered at-risk cats when I can and believe compassion for others belongs at the center of how we build the future.

Interests

  • Web development and semantic HTML
  • Data visualization and storytelling with data
  • Photography
  • Animal welfare volunteering
